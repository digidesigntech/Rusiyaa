Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We're here to help. Find answers to common queries about our products, services, and more.

What is RUSIYAA?

RUSIYAA is a trusted FMCG brand owned by Arul's Foods, committed to delivering quality food products that combine authentic taste, consistent quality, and value for customers.

Where are RUSIYAA products manufactured?

RUSIYAA products are sourced from approved and licensed manufacturing partners. Every product undergoes quality checks before being packed and marketed under the RUSIYAA brand.

Are RUSIYAA products FSSAI approved?

Yes. All RUSIYAA products comply with applicable FSSAI regulations and are sourced from licensed food manufacturing facilities.

How do I verify the authenticity of a RUSIYAA product?

Customers can verify product authenticity through the product batch details and authentication systems provided by RUSIYAA. Additional verification features may be introduced on selected products.

What products does RUSIYAA currently offer?

RUSIYAA currently offers a range of spices and masala products, including Chilli Powder, Coriander Powder, Turmeric Powder, Garam Masala, Chicken 65 and other specialty blends.

Will RUSIYAA introduce more products in the future?

Yes. RUSIYAA plans to expand its portfolio across multiple FMCG categories, including spices, masalas, food products, snacks, staples, and other household essentials.

How can I become a distributor or wholesale partner?

You can submit your details through the Distributor Enquiry section on our website or contact our sales team directly. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you.

How can retailers stock RUSIYAA products?

Retailers, supermarkets, provision stores, and wholesalers can contact us through the website or customer support channels for product availability and business enquiries.

How should RUSIYAA products be stored?

For best results, store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Always keep the pack tightly sealed after opening to maintain freshness and aroma.

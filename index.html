Our story

Every pack carries a promise.

RUSIYAA is the signature brand of Arul's Foods — built to bring traditional flavours, premium quality and modern convenience to everyday kitchens. But the story behind the brand began long before the first pack was ever sealed.

  1. The beginning

    A dream, and the will to chase it

    Our founder, Mr. Arul, started his career as an employee at a petrol bunk — carried by a simple belief that no job was too small and no dream too big.

  2. 35 years

    A lifetime learning food

    Through dedication and discipline he built a remarkable journey in hospitality, gaining invaluable experience in food preparation, quality, customer preferences, and the importance of serving people with honesty.

  3. The decision

    Turning passion into a brand

    As retirement approached, he set out to turn his lifelong passion for food into a business of his own — so families could experience the same rich flavours and quality he had spent decades perfecting.

  4. An unexpected loss

    Before the dream could begin

    Plans were made and recipes were refined. But before he could see his dream become reality, he passed away unexpectedly.

  5. What could have been the end of a dream became the beginning of a promise.

  6. The promise

    Carried forward by family

    As his daughter and son-in-law, we chose to carry his vision forward. This is more than a brand — it is a tribute to a father, mentor and hardworking man whose values continue to guide us every day.

  7. Today

    RUSIYAA — Best in Taste

    Through our flagship brand, the family continues to share authentic taste and trusted quality with households across India. We are not just selling spices — we are continuing a legacy, with every home we serve.
